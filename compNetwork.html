Types of networks and network connections

A computer network is created when two or more computers can communicate with each other.Networks are categorized by the technology used and size of the network.networked are categorized by their size or physical area they cover,these are categories used,listed from small to large

  • PAN.A PAN (personal area network consist of personal devices such as cell phones and laptop computers communicating at a close range. Can use wired (USB or Lightning) or wireless (Bluetooth or WiFi) connections
  • LAN.A LAN (local area network) covers a small local area such as home,office or small group buildings.Can use wired or wireless and allows workstations,servers,printers and other devices to communicate and share resources
  • WMN.A WMN (wireless mesh network) consist of many wireless devices communicating directly with each other rather than through a single,central device.All devices on WMN can serve as a connection point for other devices to communicate across longer distance.This technology is commonly used IoT (internet of things) wireless networks,where many types of devices such as thermostats,light switches,door locks and security cameras,are connected to the network and on to the network
  • MAN.A MAN ( metropolitan area network ) covers multiple buildings in a large campus or a portion of a city.It's usually the result of cooperative effort to improve service to it's users.Network technologies used can be wireless or wired
  • WAN.A WAN ( wide area network) covers a large geographical area and is made up of many smaller networks.Some technologies that connect a single computer or LAN to the internet include DSL,cable internet,satallite,cellular WAN and fiber optic
    • Internet connection technologies

    The most common type for SOHO network are DSL and cale internet ( cable modem ). When connecting to an ISP, know that the upload speeds are generally slower than download speed.These rates differ because users typically download more data than they upload.ISP devotes more of the available bandwidth to downloading and less of it to upload.

    Broadband technology used to connect to the internet.This technology connects a dedicated line from ISP to your place of business or home.This dedicated line is called a point-to-point(PTP) connection because no others business or residence share the line with you.TV,internet data and voice communication all share the bradband fiber-optic cable , which reaches all the way from the ISP to your home.The provider might install fiber-optic cabling up to your neighborhood and then run coaxial cable for the last leg of the connectionto your business or residence.Upstream and downstream speeds ans prices vary
