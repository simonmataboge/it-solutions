Exploring a Desktop

  • It is important for technicians to stay organized,keep notes and follow all the safety procedures to protect the computer equipment and most importantly themselves whenever they disassemble or reassemble a computer
  • Before opening a computer,shut down the whole system,disconnect all cables,press power button to drain all the power
  • Technician toolbox should include tools like ESD strap, screwdrivers, flashlight, tweezers, compressed air and cleaning solutions
  • Special tools might include POST diagnostic card tester, power supply tester, multimiter and loopback plugs
  • Technician should be familiar with computer components like Motherboard, hard drive, processor, DIMM memory module, expansion slot, power supply, optical drive
  • Computer video cards might include VGA, DVI, Display port and HDMI, other ports include RJ-45, audio, SPDIF, USB, eSATA, PS2 serial, RJ-11 ports, Thunderbolt port
  • Cases, power supply, motherboards use ATX amd Micro ATX form factors.The form factor determine which motherboard, power supply and cables fit into a case and other standards
  • Power connectors include the old 20-pin P1.24-pin P1,4-pin,and 8-pin CPU auxiliary motherboard, 4-pinMolex, 15-pin SATA and 6/8-pin PCIe connectorts
  • An expansion card fit into a slot and is anchored by a single screw or clip to the case
  • The BIOS/UEFI is responsible for keyboard, mouse hard drive,and monitor before the the OS launches to start a computer and manages the motherboard settings
  • Most hard drives and optical drives today use SATA standards to interface with the motherboard and power supply

    • Laptop components

  • Laptops are deigned for travelling,they use the same technology as desktop but they are modified to be portable, modified for space,and power conservation.A laptoppcost more than desktop with comparable power and features
  • Laptops use funtions keys to control display, volume, touch pad, media options, GPS,airline mode and other features.A laptop docking station or port replicator can make it easy to disconnect all peripheral devices
  • You can use USB ports for expansion ( you can add a USB to RJ-45 dongle, a USB to Wi-Fi dongle, Bluetooh capabilities or USB optical drive and external drive
  • Laptop manufacturer documentation includes service manual, user manual, diagnostic software recovery media, useful for disassembling, troubleshooting and repairing laptop
  • Field repleable unit(FRU) in a laptop can include, Hard drive, DIMM Memory module, LCD panel, video inverter, keyboard, touch pad, processor optical drive, DC jack, fan, motherboard, CMOS bettery, MiniPCIe card wireless card and speakers
  • When replacing components in a laptop,consider thw warranty of the alptop before opening it or not using components authorized by manufaturer
  • Replacing a laptop might be cheaper than replacing motherboard
  • When disabling a laptop,service manual is essential
  • When uprading a laptop components,use components are the same brand as laptop or recommended by manufacturer
  • Follow the instruction of service manual when you disassemble a laptop,keep your screws organized and label them,some laptops use plastic screws and it is essential to replace them rather than reusing them.Special concerns when supporting a laptop also apply to supporting a netbook or all-in-one computer
